# Product Specification

## Product

Lead.AI WhatsApp Agent

## Purpose

Lead.AI helps businesses respond to incoming WhatsApp inquiries, qualify leads, answer common questions, and hand conversations to a human when required.

## Core Goals

* Respond to inbound WhatsApp messages.
* Qualify potential leads.
* Detect booking or purchase intent.
* Escalate complex conversations to a human.
* Store CRM-ready lead information.

## MVP Scope

* WhatsApp webhook handling.
* Lead qualification workflow.
* FAQ responses.
* Human handoff rules.
* Basic lead storage.

## Out Of Scope

* Multi-channel messaging.
* Advanced CRM integrations.
* Autonomous sales decisions.
* Voice interactions.
